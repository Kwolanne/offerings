# offerings
This is a space to share digital offerings to the planet and people you love. Use the text box to write a personal message addressed to your loved one or place, and select items from the general list.

<h3>What are offerings</h3>
<p>Across a range of cultures, offerings are gifts that carry spiritual significance, often to deities, dead loved ones, or a particular element of nature. These can be libations to honor a spiritual being, keepsakes that celebrate a loved one who has passed, and culturally specific materials dedicated to land or waterways you are deeply connected to. If you're not sure what to give, do research on your own cultural background and common offering traditions. For offerings to nature, look into local traditions that don't negatively impact local ecologies. </p>
